B I N G O

How To Play: Grab a friend/multiple friends. Fill your boxes with your desired order, take turns selecting the desired number. For every row/vertical/diagonal that has 5 numbers selected, you get one letter of B I N G O, once you have all 5 letters, you win!

Fill your 5x5 grid with 1-25 in any order by simply clicking the boxes to set it up, OR randomise it for a quick game
